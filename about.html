We started with small, local businesses in Bahawalpur — a clothing brand trying to grow beyond its shop, a home-based bakery that needed an online presence, and a local café wanting visibility on Google.

These weren't just early clients — they were our classroom. We built websites, ran ad campaigns, improved their SEO, and created brand identities that truly resonated. Those experiences taught us what works, and more importantly, why it works.

Today, TezRank has grown into a full-service agency — but we've never forgotten our roots. Every client, big or small, gets the same dedication we gave to those first businesses.
