Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?
+

A standard business website takes 1–2 weeks from brief to launch. Complex e-commerce or custom sites may take 3–4 weeks. We always share a clear timeline before starting.

How soon will I see SEO results?
+

SEO is long-term. Most clients see measurable improvements in 2–3 months, with significant ranking changes in 4–6 months. Monthly reports keep you updated on every step.

What is the minimum budget for digital ads?
+

We recommend a minimum ad spend of PKR 30,000–50,000/month for Facebook/Instagram. For Google Ads, PKR 40,000/month is a good start. Our management fee is separate and fully transparent.

Is the mentorship online or in-person?
+

Available both online (Zoom/Google Meet) and in-person in Bahawalpur. Sessions are practical and project-based — you learn by working on real campaigns, not just theory.

Can I bundle multiple services together?
+

Absolutely! Bundling Web Development + SEO + Digital Marketing is our most popular combination and often comes with a discounted rate. Many clients start with one service and expand as they grow.
