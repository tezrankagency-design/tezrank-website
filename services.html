Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?
+

A standard business website typically takes 1–2 weeks from brief to launch. More complex e-commerce or custom sites may take 3–4 weeks. We always share a clear timeline before starting.

How soon will I see SEO results?
+

SEO is a long-term strategy. Most clients start seeing measurable improvements in 2–3 months, with significant ranking changes in 4–6 months. We provide monthly reports so you can track every step.

What is the minimum budget for digital ads?
+

We recommend a minimum ad spend of PKR 30,000–50,000/month for meaningful results on Facebook/Instagram. For Google Ads, we suggest starting at PKR 40,000/month. Our management fee is separate and transparent.

Is the mentorship program online or in-person?
+

Our mentorship is available both online (via Zoom/Google Meet) and in-person in Bahawalpur. Sessions are practical and project-based — you learn by doing real work, not just theory.

Do you offer packages or custom pricing?
+

We offer both! Standard packages are available for common needs, but we also build fully custom proposals based on your business goals, industry, and budget. Contact us for a free consultation.

Can I bundle multiple services together?
+

Absolutely. Bundling Web Development + SEO + Digital Marketing is our most popular combination and often comes with a discounted rate. Many clients start with one service and expand as they grow.
